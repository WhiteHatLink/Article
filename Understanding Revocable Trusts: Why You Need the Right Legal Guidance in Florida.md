What Is a Revocable Trust?

A revocable trust—sometimes known as a “living trust”—is a legal arrangement that allows you to manage your assets during your lifetime and ensure they are distributed according to your wishes after your passing. The key feature is flexibility: as the grantor, you can change, amend, or revoke the trust at any time.

This flexibility makes a revocable trust especially appealing for individuals who want to retain control of their property while simplifying the transfer process later. However, setting up a trust involves important legal considerations, which is why working with professional revocable trust lawyers in Brandon, FL ensures that your documents are drafted correctly and comply with Florida’s trust laws.

Why Florida Residents Choose Revocable Trusts

Florida’s probate process can be lengthy and costly. By establishing a revocable trust, your assets can bypass probate entirely, saving your loved ones time, expense, and potential stress. Additionally, revocable trusts maintain privacy—unlike wills, which become public record during probate proceedings.
