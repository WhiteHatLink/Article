<p><strong>Why Do Tile Roofs Get Damaged?</strong></p>
<p>Tile roofs are tough, but they are not unbreakable. Here are some common reasons tiles may need repairs:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Falling Branches:</strong> Trees near the house can drop heavy branches that crack tiles.</li>
<li><strong>Weather Changes:</strong> Strong winds, hail, and heavy rain can loosen or shift tiles.</li>
<li><strong>Aging:</strong> Old tiles may weaken, break, or slide out of place.</li>
<li><strong>Walking on Roofs:</strong> Stepping on tiles without care can cause cracks.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>What Happens If You Ignore Broken Tiles?</strong></p>
<p>Many people think one small crack will not harm the roof, but that is not true. Ignoring broken tiles can lead to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Water stains on ceilings</li>
<li>Damp walls and peeling paint</li>
<li>Growth of mold, which is bad for health</li>
<li>Weakening of the roof structure</li>
</ul>
<p>Small repairs done early can stop these bigger problems and save a lot of money.</p>
<p><strong>How Are Tile Roofs Repaired?</strong></p>
<p>Repairing a tile roof depends on the damage. Some common methods are:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Replacing Broken Tiles:</strong> Old or cracked tiles are removed and replaced with new ones.</li>
<li><strong>Re-Sealing:</strong> Roofers use sealant to stop water from passing through small cracks.</li>
<li><strong>Checking Underlay:</strong> The layer under the tiles is checked for leaks or holes.</li>
<li><strong>Cleaning Gutters:</strong> A full repair often includes gutters, because blocked gutters can push water back under tiles.</li>
</ul>
<p>This shows why roof and gutter repairs Brisbane often go together. Both must work properly to keep water away from the house.</p>
<p><strong>When Should You Call for Repairs?</strong></p>
<p>You should not wait until the ceiling starts dripping. Some early signs that you need a roofer are:</p>
<ul>
<li>Tiles sliding out of place</li>
<li>Cracks you can see from the ground</li>
<li>Gutters overflowing in rain</li>
<li>Strange damp smells inside the house</li>
</ul>
<p>Getting repairs at the right time protects your home from bigger problems later.</p>
