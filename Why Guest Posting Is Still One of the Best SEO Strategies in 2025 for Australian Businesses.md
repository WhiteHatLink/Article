<p><strong>What Is Guest Posting?</strong></p>
<p>Guest posting, also called guest blogging, is when you write and publish content on another website in your niche. In return, you usually receive a backlink to your website. Unlike old-school link building methods, guest posting provides real value:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Fresh content</strong> for the host website,</li>
<li><strong>Quality backlinks</strong> for the author,</li>
<li><strong>Authority and credibility</strong> for both sides.</li>
</ul>
<p>In 2025, search engines reward websites that build natural, contextual backlinks &mdash; and guest posting is one of the best ways to achieve this.</p>
