<p><strong>Understanding Flow Measurement</strong></p>
<p>Flow measurement means calculating how much fluid passes through a pipe over a certain time. This fluid can be liquid or gas.</p>
<p>There are two main types of flow measurement:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong> Volumetric Flow</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>This measures the volume of fluid, like liters per minute.</p>
<ol start="2">
<li><strong> Mass Flow</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>This measures the mass of fluid, like kilograms per hour.</p>
<p>Mass flow is often more accurate because it is not affected by temperature or pressure changes.</p>
<p><strong>Why Accurate Flow Measurement Is Important</strong></p>
<p>Accuracy is very important in industrial applications. Even small errors can cause big problems over time.</p>
<p><strong>Cost Efficiency</strong></p>
<p>Incorrect measurement can lead to overuse or underuse of materials. This increases production costs.</p>
<p><strong>Safety</strong></p>
<p>Wrong flow readings can cause pressure build-up or leaks, which can be dangerous.</p>
<p><strong>Product Quality</strong></p>
<p>In industries like food and chemicals, the exact quantity of ingredients is necessary.</p>
<p><strong>Regulatory Compliance</strong></p>
<p>Many industries must follow strict standards. Accurate measurement helps meet these rules.</p>

<p><strong>Thermal Mass Flow Meters</strong></p>
<p>The <strong>Medidor de flujo m&aacute;sico t&eacute;rmico</strong> is designed for measuring gas flow.</p>
<p><strong>Working Principle</strong></p>
<p>It measures the heat transfer from a heated sensor. When gas flows over the sensor, it cools it down. The cooling effect shows the flow rate.</p>
<p><strong>Key Features</strong></p>
<ul>

<li>No need for pressure correction</li>
<li>Easy installation</li>
